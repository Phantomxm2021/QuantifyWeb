Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

Welcome to Quantify ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use the Quantify mobile application (the "App") and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together follows:

  • Identity Data: includes username or similar identifier, if you create an account.
  • Image Data: includes screenshots or images of financial charts that you upload for analysis.
  • Usage Data: includes information about how you use our App, such as button taps and feature usage.
  • Device Data: includes internet protocol (IP) address, device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To Provide Services: To process your chart images using Google Gemini AI and return technical analysis strategies.
  • To Improve Our App: To understand how users interact with the App and identify areas for improvement.
  • To Manage Your Account: To manage your subscription and user profile.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Google Gemini AI: We send the chart images you upload to Google's Gemini API for analysis. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.
  • Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication and database services.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions.

4. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Chart images uploaded for analysis are typically processed in real-time and are not permanently stored by us unless you choose to save them to your history.

5. Your Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, erasure, restriction, transfer, to object to processing, to portability of data and (where the lawful ground of processing is consent) to withdraw consent.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: nswell@phantomsxr.com
